Assessment and Certification

Certification Levels

Level 1 (Basic)

Basic operations under supervision

Level 2 (Intermediate)

Routine operations independently

Level 3 (Advanced)

Complex operations and guidance

Level 4 (Expert)

Develop techniques and provide training

Certification Validity

Initial certification valid for 2 years, with recertification required to maintain currency

Assessment Methodologies

Continuing Education

  • Quarterly legal and technical updates
  • Annual refresher training (16 hours)
  • Peer review and operational feedback
  • Specialized workshops for emerging threats

Professional Accreditation

Program designed to meet standards for accreditation by:

  • College of Policing (Digital Investigation)
  • National Police Chiefs' Council
  • Information Commissioner's Office

Rigorous Assessment Ensures Operational Readiness
